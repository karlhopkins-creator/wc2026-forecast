Unlocks when all 72 group games are entered. Then tap actual winners to lock results; unplayed rounds stay as live odds above.

Method. Per simulation, entered scores are used as-is and unplayed matches get correlated scorelines from a Dixon-Coles adjusted Poisson model, with expected goals set by the Elo gap. Each simulation also re-draws every team's rating from a distribution around its Elo, so the model never pretends to know a team's strength exactly and the tails stay honest. Group tables resolve on points, goal difference, goals scored. The eight best third-placed teams are slotted into the official Round-of-32 positions (constraint-matched, valid across all 495 combinations). Knockout ties you've locked are forced; the rest are simulated. Odds are the share of simulations in which each outcome occurs.

Notes. Seed Elo is an editable approximate snapshot — refresh from eloratings.net. Real third-place and group tiebreakers add discipline and head-to-head, approximated here. Share links encode ratings, scores and locks in the URL.
